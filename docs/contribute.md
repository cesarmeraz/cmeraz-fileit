# Contribute

Assuming that you're a member of my workplace and want to participate in our hackathon, here are the steps to join the team:

1. Email Laura and Kalim and let them know you want to join the Service Bus project, Lab 34, and CC me.
2. Read the docs, assign yourself an issue to work on, make a branch from main and try it out locally.
3. Create a PR when you're done and ask me to review.
4. Send me an [email](mailto:cesar.meraz@gmail.com) mentioning what nights you'd be free to meet and/or what days we could meet for lunch.
